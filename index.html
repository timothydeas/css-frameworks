Peru meets Miami!

Raul grew up in Cuzco, Peru where he and his family would harvest fresh ingredients to cook in ovens made from mother earth. These ovens were made with clay and hot coals and the ingredients were wrapped and baked for thirty to fourty minutes. This process developed a natural oak taste that brought everything to perfection. What was created in those childhood clay oven days has lived with Raul as he came to America and hence has created an infusion of Peruvian and American ingredients to leave one with this same lifetime of memories to carry on. Please view our menu and join us in making your memories too!
